Empowering Farmers with Knowledge and Resources

At Shamba Salama, we believe in empowering farmers with the knowledge and resources they need to thrive. Our mobile agricultural extension service provides convenient access to expert advice, market information, and essential agricultural resources, right at your fingertips.

Why Choose Shamba Salama?

  • Expert Guidance: Get personalized advice and support from agricultural specialists and extension agents.
  • Timely Information: Stay informed with up-to-date agricultural news, weather forecasts, and market prices.
  • Empowering Farmers: Access resources and tools to improve farming practices, increase productivity, and enhance livelihoods.
  • Convenient Access: Whether you're in the field or at home, our service is accessible anytime, anywhere, via mobile app, SMS, or website.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Register for our service to start receiving agricultural information and support.
  2. Explore Resources: Access a wealth of information on crop management, livestock care, pest control, and more.
  3. Connect with Experts: Seek advice from experienced agricultural specialists and extension agents.
  4. Stay Informed: Receive timely alerts and updates on weather conditions, market prices, and farming tips.
  5. Grow Your Farm: Implement best practices and strategies to optimize your farm's productivity and profitability.
